Mutton Biryani

Mutton biryani is a popular and flavorful dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is a type of biryani, a dish made with basmati rice, spices, and either meat, vegetables, or both. Mutton biryani specifically features tender pieces of mutton (goat meat or lamb), cooked with aromatic basmati rice, and a blend of spices that give it its rich and savory flavor.

Prawn Biryani

Prawns biryani is a delectable and aromatic dish that blends the rich flavors of biryani with the delicate sweetness of prawns. It is a seafood variant of the traditional biryani, a popular rice dish in South Asian cuisine. Prawns biryani is known for its fragrant basmati rice cooked with juicy, succulent prawns, and a medley of spices that create a harmonious balance of flavors.
